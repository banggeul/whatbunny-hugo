In this video, the artist reenacts the seminal 60’s video performance pieces by Bruce Nauman with the intention of loosing weight. The video questions the process of art production, specifically the enduring ‘mythology’ around originality and its associated value while the texts with apparent marked corrections give the artist’s process of rather insecure and shallow decision-makings a material form.
