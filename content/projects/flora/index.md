FLORA (Feminist Ledger of Reproductive Access) is a decorative tablecloth patterned with flowers and plants historically used in reproductive health, both as contraceptives and abortifacients. Along its edges, hundreds of hand-embroidered LEDs form a flexible display that presents continuously updated, current legislative data on reproductive rights, with the scrolling text distorted by the fabric’s draping folds.

R&D by Craftwork Collective
