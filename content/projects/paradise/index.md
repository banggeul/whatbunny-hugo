A gentle female voice emanates from a commercially available fake rock designed to conceal garden fixtures. Relying upon custom software and a micro-controller hidden inside the rock, the voice reads aloud the over twenty thousand names of US-based financial officers revealed in the Paradise Papers, one after the other, with the voice quietly chuckling and moaning in between the names.
